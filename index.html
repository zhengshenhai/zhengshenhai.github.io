Biography

I am Shenhai Zheng. I received my Ph.D. degree from the College of Computer Science, Chongqing University. I am currently a professor at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications (CQUPT). Our main research directions are intelligent computing, pattern recognition, image processing and machine vision.
